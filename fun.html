About Me

DS

Dedicated and accomplished academic with over 20 years of experience in computer science education. I bring proven expertise in mobile cloud computing, data analytics, and machine learning to both my teaching and research endeavors.

My journey has been recognized nationally and internationally through multiple awards and certifications. I am deeply committed to empowering students and enhancing educational practices through innovative teaching methodologies and meaningful research contributions.

As Head of Computer Science at Sri GVG Visalakshi College for Women, I continue to shape the next generation of computer scientists while pushing the boundaries of research in emerging technologies.
